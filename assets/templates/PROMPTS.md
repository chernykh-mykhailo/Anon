# Image Template Prompts

Use these prompts to generate more backgrounds for the Valentin card system. 
**Key rule:** Always ensure there is a large, clean empty space in the center for text overlay.

### 1. Romantic Pink (Watercolors)
`A romantic pastel pink background with soft watercolor hearts around the edges. Large empty clean space in the middle for text. High-quality aesthetic card style. 1024x1024.`

### 2. Luxury Red (Premium)
`Classic deep red luxury background with subtle gold heart patterns on the borders. Solid empty dark red space in the center for clear text readability. 1024x1024.`

### 3. Dreamy Gradient (Modern)
`Aesthetic soft blue and purple gradient background with dreamlike floating white hearts at the bottom. Wide empty space in the upper and middle part for text. 1024x1024.`

### 4. Retro Kraft (Vintage)
`Vintage kraft paper texture with small dried red flowers in the corners. Clean center area with no objects for handwriting-style text. 1024x1024.`

### 5. Minimalist White
`Pure minimalist white background with a single elegant line-art heart in a corner. Massive clean white space for text. High-end stationery feel. 1024x1024.`
